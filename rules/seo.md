# SEO Rules（SEO规则）


## Purpose（目的）


These rules define SEO standards for Amazon Affiliate Review Websites.

这些规则用于规范 Amazon 联盟测评网站的 SEO 标准。


The goal:

目标：

- Rank higher on Google（提升Google排名）
- Get organic traffic（获得自然流量）
- Build topical authority（建立主题权威）
- Increase affiliate conversions（提高联盟转化）


---

# SEO Strategy（SEO策略）


Every page must focus on:

每个页面必须围绕：


1. Search Intent（搜索意图）

2. User Experience（用户体验）

3. Content Quality（内容质量）

4. E-E-A-T（经验、专业、权威、可信）

5. Conversion Optimization（转化优化）


Never create content only for keywords.

禁止只为了关键词生成内容。


---

# Keyword Research Rules（关键词研究）


Every page requires:


## Primary Keyword（核心关键词）


Only one main keyword.

每个页面只能有一个核心关键词。


Example:

Product Name Review


---

## Secondary Keywords（辅助关键词）


Include:

包含：

3-8 related keywords


Example:


Product Name specs

Product Name top speed

Product Name battery

Product Name range


---

## LSI Keywords（相关语义词）


Add related terms naturally.


Example:


electric dirt bike

off road motorcycle

hydraulic suspension

fat tire ebike


---

# Search Intent Analysis（搜索意图）


Before creating content:

创建内容之前必须分析：


User wants:


## Informational

用户想了解：

"What is..."


Create:

Educational content


---

## Commercial Investigation

用户准备购买：

"Best"

"Review"

"Vs"


Create:

Review / Comparison pages


---

## Transactional

用户准备购买：

"Buy"

"Price"


Create:

Conversion focused pages


---

# SEO Title Rules（标题规则）


Every page needs:


SEO Title:

55-60 characters


Must include:

包含：

Primary keyword


Example:


Product Name Review: Specs, Performance & Real Testing


---

# Meta Description Rules（描述规则）


Length:

140-160 characters


Include:

包含：

- Main keyword
- User benefit
- CTA


Example:


Complete Product Name review covering specs, performance, price and whether this product is worth buying.


---

# URL Rules（URL规则）


URLs must be:


- Short
- Clean
- Keyword relevant


Correct:


/product-name-review


Wrong:


/article?id=123


---

# Content Structure Rules（内容结构）


Every important page should include:


## Introduction

Explain:

- What product is
- Who it is for
- Main conclusion


---

## Quick Verdict

Provide:

- Rating
- Main advantages
- Main disadvantages


---

## Main Content

Include:

Specifications

Features

Performance

Experience

Comparison


---

## FAQ

Answer:

Real user questions.


---

# E-E-A-T Rules（Google可信度规则）


Every review should demonstrate:


## Experience（经验）


Include:


Testing process

Real usage scenario

Performance evaluation


---

## Expertise（专业）


Explain:

Why this product performs well or poorly.


---

## Authority（权威）


Use:

- Manufacturer information
- Industry standards
- Certification information


---

## Trust（可信）


Include:


- Pros
- Cons
- Limitations
- Disclosure


---

# Internal Linking Rules（内链规则）


Every article must contain:


Link to:

至少链接：


- Homepage

- Related reviews

- Comparison pages

- Buying guides


Anchor text must be descriptive.


Example:


Good:

Best electric dirt bikes


Bad:

Click here


---

# External Link Rules（外链规则）


Only link to:


- Official websites
- Government sources
- Certification organizations


Avoid:

Low-quality websites


---

# Schema Rules（结构化数据）


Use when applicable:


Product Schema

Review Schema

FAQ Schema

Article Schema

Breadcrumb Schema


---

# Featured Snippet Optimization（精选摘要）


Content should target:


Google snippets:


Use:


Question headings

Short answers

Tables

Lists


Example:


## How fast is Product Name?


Answer directly within 40-60 words.


---

# Image SEO Rules（图片SEO）


Every image needs:


Filename:


Correct:

product-name-category.webp


Wrong:

IMG001.webp


---

ALT:


Describe image naturally.


Example:


Product Name product main view


---

# Affiliate SEO Rules（联盟SEO）


Commercial pages must include:


- Buying recommendation
- CTA button
- Alternative products
- Honest review


Do not:

Overpromise results.


---

# AI Content Rules（AI内容规则）


AI generated content must be:


Edited

Improved

Fact checked


Never publish raw AI content.


---

# Google Helpful Content Rules（有用内容原则）


Every page must answer:


What is it?

Who should buy?

Who should avoid?

Is it worth the money?

What are alternatives?


---

# Final SEO Principle（SEO最终原则）


Create the most useful page for users.

创建互联网中最有价值的页面。


Ranking is the result of quality.

排名来自内容价值。
