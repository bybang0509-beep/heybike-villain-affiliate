# Affiliate Rules（联盟营销规则）


## Purpose（目的）


These rules define standards for Amazon Affiliate website monetization.

这些规则用于规范 Amazon 联盟网站的商业化方式。


The purpose is NOT to maximize clicks.

目的不是单纯追求点击。


The purpose is:

目标是：

Help users make confident purchase decisions and naturally generate affiliate conversions.

帮助用户做出有信心的购买决定，并自然产生联盟转化。


---

# Affiliate Philosophy（联盟理念）


An affiliate website should behave like:

联盟网站应该像：


A trusted product advisor.

一个值得信任的产品顾问。


NOT:

不是：

A sales page.

一个销售页面。


---

# User Trust First（用户信任优先）


Every recommendation must answer:


每个推荐必须回答：


Why this product?

为什么推荐这个产品？


Who is it for?

适合谁？


Who should avoid it?

谁不应该买？


What are the limitations?

有什么不足？


---

# Recommendation Rules（推荐规则）


Products must be recommended based on:


产品推荐依据：


- User needs（用户需求）
- Performance（性能）
- Quality（质量）
- Value（价值）
- Reliability（可靠性）


Never recommend products only because:


禁止因为以下原因推荐：


- Higher commission（佣金更高）
- Higher price（价格更高）
- Brand request（品牌要求）


---

# Affiliate Disclosure（联盟声明）


Every commercial page should include affiliate disclosure.


所有商业页面必须包含联盟声明。


Example:


"This website participates in affiliate programs. We may earn a commission when you purchase through our links, at no additional cost to you."


Rules:


Disclosure must be:


- Clear
- Easy to find
- Honest


Do not hide disclosures.


---

# CTA Strategy（行动按钮策略）


CTA means:

Call To Action


行动按钮不是强迫购买。


CTA should guide users.


Examples:


Good:


Check Latest Price


View Product Details


See Current Amazon Price


Read Customer Reviews


Avoid:


Buy Now!!!


Limited Time!!!


Only Today!!!


unless verified.


---

# CTA Placement（CTA位置）


Recommended locations:


## 1. Quick Verdict


After explaining the conclusion.


Reason:

用户已经了解产品价值。


---

## 2. Product Recommendation Card


After:


- Product summary
- Best for
- Pros
- Cons


---

## 3. Comparison Table


After users understand differences.


---

## 4. Final Verdict


At the end of article.


---

# Product Card Rules（产品卡片）


Every product card should include:


Product name


Image


Short description


Best for


Main advantages


Main disadvantages


Rating


CTA button


---

Example:


Product Name


★★★★★ 4.5/5


Best for:

Beginner users


Pros:

✓ Easy to use


Cons:

✗ Higher price


Button:

Check Price


---

# Conversion Optimization（转化优化）


Improve conversions by:


提高转化：


## Provide Decision Support


帮助用户决策。


Include:


- Comparison
- Pros and Cons
- Alternatives
- Buying advice


---

## Reduce User Doubt


降低用户疑虑。


Answer:


Is it reliable?


Is it worth the money?


What problems may happen?


---

## Make Information Easy To Find


让信息容易找到。


Use:


Tables

Bullet points

Summary boxes

FAQ


---

# Avoid Aggressive Selling（避免强推）


Do not use:


不要使用：


"This is the best product ever."


"The only product you need."


"Guaranteed perfect."


Use:


"This may be a good choice for..."


"Suitable for users who..."


---

# Honest Review Requirements（真实评价要求）


Every promoted product should include:


Advantages:


产品优势。


Limitations:


产品限制。


Ideal users:


适合人群。


Alternative options:


替代选择。


---

# Commercial Content Balance（商业内容平衡）


Recommended balance:


70% User Value

30% Product Recommendation


Do not make the entire page a sales pitch.


---

# Amazon Link Rules（Amazon链接规则）


Links should appear naturally.


Good:


After explaining benefits.


Bad:


Every paragraph contains links.


Avoid excessive affiliate links.


---

# Trust Signals（信任信号）


Add:


- Review methodology
- Evaluation criteria
- Author information
- Update date
- Sources


---

# Pricing Rules（价格规则）


Prices change frequently.


Avoid fixed claims.


Use:


"Check latest price"


"Current Amazon price"


when necessary.


---

# Negative Review Handling（负面评价处理）


Do not hide common complaints.


Explain:


- What users dislike
- Whether problems affect everyone
- Possible solutions


Balanced reviews build trust.


---

# User Intent Matching（匹配购买意图）


Different pages need different CTA:


## Review Page:


User wants:

"Should I buy?"


CTA:

Check Price


---

## Comparison Page:


User wants:

"Which is better?"


CTA:

View Best Option


---

## Buying Guide:


User wants:

"What should I choose?"


CTA:

See Recommended Products


---

# Google Quality Rules（Google质量规则）


Avoid:


- Thin affiliate pages
- Product copy from Amazon
- Fake reviews
- Fake testing claims
- Excessive ads


The page must provide additional value beyond Amazon.


---

# Final Principle（最终原则）


The goal of affiliate marketing is not to make users click.


The goal is to help users make the right decision.


正确的联盟营销不是让用户点击链接。


而是让用户相信：

"这个网站真的帮助我选择了正确产品。"
