# Content Rules（内容编辑规则）


## Purpose（目的）


These rules define content creation standards for Amazon Affiliate Websites.

这些规则用于规范 Amazon 联盟网站内容生产标准。


The goal is NOT to create more content.

目标不是制造更多文章。


The goal is:

目标是：


Create the most helpful content that helps users make better buying decisions.

创建真正帮助用户做出更好购买决定的内容。


---

# Content Philosophy（内容理念）


Write like:

像：

A product expert helping a friend choose the right product.

一个懂产品的专家帮助朋友选择正确产品。


Do NOT write like:

不要像：

A salesperson trying to sell a product.

一个只想卖货的销售人员。


---

# User First Principle（用户优先原则）


Before writing any article, understand:


写任何文章之前，必须理解：


## User Intent（用户目的）


Why is the user searching?


用户为什么搜索？


Examples:


"I want to know"

我想了解


"Is it worth buying?"

值得买吗？


"Which one should I choose?"

我该选哪个？


"How to avoid mistakes?"

如何避免踩坑？


---

# Buyer Pain Point Research（购买痛点研究）


Every article must identify:


每篇文章必须分析：


## 1. User Problems（用户问题）


What problem does the user want to solve?


用户想解决什么问题？


Example:


"I need a bike for commuting."


---

## 2. User Concerns（用户担忧）


Common concerns:


用户常见担忧：


- Is it reliable?
- Is it worth the money?
- Will it last?
- Is it easy to use?
- Are there hidden problems?


---

## 3. Buying Risks（购买风险）


Explain:


- Possible disadvantages
- Limitations
- Maintenance issues
- Long-term costs


---

# Content Research Rules（内容研究规则）


Before creating content, collect:


创建内容前收集：


## Product Information


Sources:


- Official website
- Amazon listing
- Manufacturer specifications


---

## User Feedback


Analyze:


- Amazon reviews
- Common complaints
- Common praises


Find:


Real user experiences.


---

## Competitor Research


Analyze:


- Top ranking pages
- Content gaps
- Missing information


Do NOT copy.


Create better content.


---

# Content Types（内容类型）


The website should create:


## Product Reviews（产品测评）


Purpose:


Help users decide whether to buy.


Must include:


- Overview
- Experience
- Pros
- Cons
- Who should buy
- Who should avoid
- Final verdict


---

## Comparison Articles（对比文章）


Purpose:


Help users choose between options.


Must include:


- Main differences
- Advantages
- Disadvantages
- Best choice for different users


---

## Buying Guides（购买指南）


Purpose:


Teach users how to choose.


Must include:


- Buying factors
- Common mistakes
- Recommendations
- User scenarios


---

## Educational Content（知识内容）


Purpose:


Build topical authority.


Examples:


- How it works
- Maintenance tips
- Beginner guides
- Industry knowledge


---

# Writing Style Rules（写作风格）


Use:


- Clear language
- Short paragraphs
- Natural tone
- Practical explanations


Avoid:


- Empty marketing words
- Over exaggeration
- Fake enthusiasm


---

# AI Writing Rules（AI写作规则）


AI generated content must be improved.


Before publishing:


Add:


- Human perspective
- Practical advice
- Real concerns
- Unique analysis


Never publish:


Raw AI content.


---

# Expert Tone（专家语气）


Write with confidence but remain honest.


Good:


"This product is a good option for users who need..."


Bad:


"This is the perfect product for everyone."


---

# Product Evaluation Rules（产品评价规则）


Every product analysis should cover:


## Strengths（优势）


Explain:


Why this product is good.


---

## Weaknesses（不足）


Explain:


Where this product may disappoint.


---

## Trade-offs（取舍）


Explain:


What users gain and what they sacrifice.


Example:


Higher performance but higher price.


---

# Content Structure Rules（文章结构）


Use:


## Introduction


Explain user problem.


## Main Analysis


Provide useful information.


## Recommendation


Give clear advice.


## Conclusion


Help users decide.


---

# Original Value Rules（原创价值）


Every article must add information beyond:


- Amazon page
- Manufacturer page


Examples:


Add:


- Buying advice
- Feature explanation
- User scenarios
- Comparison
- Common mistakes


---

# Topical Authority Strategy（主题权威）


Build content clusters.


Example:


Main Topic:

[Product Category]


Cluster:


[Product Category] Reviews

↓

[Product Category] Comparisons

↓

[Product Category] Buying Guides

↓

Maintenance / Educational Articles


---

# Internal Content Relationship（内容关联）


Every article should connect with related content.


Example:


Review:


links to


Buying Guide


Comparison


Alternative Products


---

# FAQ Content Rules（FAQ规则）


FAQ should answer real questions.


Sources:


- Google autocomplete
- People Also Ask
- Amazon customer questions


Avoid:


Fake questions.


---

# Fact Checking Rules（事实检查）


Verify:


- Specifications
- Prices
- Certifications
- Features


Never invent:


- Testing results
- Certifications
- User experience


---

# Update Rules（更新规则）


Content should be updated when:


- Product changes
- Price changes
- New competitors appear
- Information becomes outdated


Include:


Last Updated date.


---

# Conversion Through Helpfulness（通过帮助实现转化）


The best conversion strategy is:


More trust.


Trust comes from:


- Honest information
- Balanced reviews
- Clear recommendations


---

# Final Principle（最终原则）


The purpose of content is not to sell products.


The purpose of content is to help people make smarter purchases.


When users trust the website, conversions naturally happen.
