# Comparison Rules（产品对比规则）


## Purpose（目的）


These rules define standards for Amazon Affiliate comparison pages.

这些规则用于规范 Amazon 联盟网站中的产品对比页面。


The goal is to create comparison content that:

目标：

- Help users choose between products（帮助用户选择产品）
- Capture high purchase intent traffic（获取高购买意图流量）
- Improve affiliate conversion（提高联盟转化）
- Rank for comparison keywords（获得对比关键词排名）


---

# Comparison Philosophy（对比原则）


A comparison page is not a simple specification list.

对比页面不是简单罗列参数。


A good comparison must explain:


优秀对比必须解释：


- Which product is better for different users
（哪个产品适合不同用户）


- The important differences
（核心区别）


- The advantages and disadvantages
（优缺点）


- Which one provides better value
（哪个性价比更高）


---

# Search Intent（搜索意图）


Comparison pages target users close to purchase.


对比页面针对接近购买阶段的用户。


Common keywords:


Product A vs Product B


Product A or Product B


Product A alternative


Best Product A alternatives


Which is better Product A or B


---

# Page Title Rules（标题规则）


Title format:


Product A vs Product B: Which One Should You Buy?


Must include:


- Both product names
- Comparison keyword
- User benefit


Avoid:


Clickbait titles.


---

# Introduction（开头）


The introduction should:


Explain:


- What products are compared
- Who they are designed for
- Main comparison conclusion


Example:


"We compare Product A and Product B to help you decide which option fits your needs and budget."


---

# Quick Winner Summary（快速结论）


Every comparison must include:


## Overall Winner


Example:


Winner:

Product A


Reason:

Better performance and value.


---

## Best For Different Users


Example:


Best for beginners:

Product A


Best for professionals:

Product B


Best budget choice:

Product A

# Feature Difference Analysis（功能差异分析）


Do not only say:


"Product A has more features."


Explain:


Feature:

↓

Difference:

↓

User benefit:


Example:


Product A has a larger battery.


Meaning:


Users can use it longer before charging.


---

# Performance Comparison（性能对比）


Analyze:


Which product performs better?


Explain:


- Real-world advantages
- Limitations
- Usage scenarios


Avoid unsupported claims.


---

# Price Comparison（价格分析）


Do not only compare price.


Explain:


Value:


Price

+

Features

+

Quality

+

Long-term usefulness


---

# User Recommendation（用户推荐）


Every comparison must answer:


Who should buy Product A?


Who should buy Product B?


Example:


Choose Product A if:


- You need lower price
- You are a beginner


Choose Product B if:


- You need premium performance
- Budget is less important

# Alternative Section（替代选择）


When appropriate include:


Other products users may consider.


Purpose:


Capture additional search traffic.


Examples:


Similar products

Cheaper alternatives

Premium alternatives


---

# FAQ Rules（FAQ规则）


Include comparison questions:


Examples:


Which product is better?


Is Product A worth the extra money?


What is the main difference?


Which one lasts longer?


---

# Affiliate CTA Rules（联盟转化）


Add CTA naturally.


Recommended locations:


1. After winner summary


2. After comparison table


3. Final verdict


CTA examples:


Check Latest Price


View Product Details


See Current Amazon Deal


---

# Internal Linking（内链）


Link to:


Product reviews:


Product A Review

Product B Review

Best products category


Related comparisons:


Similar comparisons


---

# SEO Rules（SEO要求）


Every comparison page requires:


SEO Title


Meta Description


Schema:


- Article Schema
- FAQ Schema
- Product Schema when applicable


---

# Content Quality Rules（内容质量）


Comparison must be:


Balanced

Detailed

Evidence-based


Never:


Declare a winner without explanation.


---

# Length Guidelines（文章长度）


Recommended:


Basic comparison:

2500+ words


Deep comparison:

3500-5000 words


Quality matters more than length.


---

# Final Rule（最终规则）


The purpose of comparison content is not to promote one product.

对比文章不是为了硬推一个产品。


The purpose is:


Help users confidently choose the right product.


帮助用户做出正确购买决定。
