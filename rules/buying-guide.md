# Buying Guide Rules（购买指南规则）


## Purpose（目的）


These rules define standards for Amazon Affiliate buying guide content.

这些规则用于规范 Amazon 联盟网站购买指南内容。


The purpose is NOT to push products.

目的不是强行推荐产品。


The purpose is:


Help buyers understand their needs, avoid mistakes, and choose the right product.

帮助购买者理解自己的需求，避免踩坑，并选择真正适合自己的产品。


---

# Buyer First Principle（购买者优先原则）


Always think from the buyer's perspective.


始终站在购买者角度思考。


Before recommending anything, answer:


在推荐产品之前，必须回答：


- What problem does the buyer want to solve?
（用户想解决什么问题）


- What are their biggest concerns?
（用户最担心什么）


- What mistakes do beginners usually make?
（新手容易犯什么错误）


- What features actually matter?
（哪些参数真正重要）


- What features are only marketing?
（哪些只是营销噱头）


---

# Buyer Pain Point Analysis（购买痛点分析）


Every buying guide must identify:


## 1. Budget Concerns（预算）


Users care about:


- Price
- Long-term value
- Maintenance cost
- Hidden costs


Explain:


Cheap does not always mean good value.

Expensive does not always mean better.


---

## 2. Quality Concerns（质量）


Users worry about:


- Durability
- Material quality
- Reliability
- Brand reputation


Explain:


What affects product lifespan.


---

## 3. Performance Concerns（性能）


Users want to know:


Does it perform well in real situations?


Analyze:


- Speed
- Power
- Battery
- Capacity
- Efficiency


---

## 4. Compatibility Concerns（适合性）


Users ask:


"Is this right for me?"


Always explain:


Good for:


- Beginners
- Professionals
- Families
- Specific scenarios


Not suitable for:


- Certain users
- Certain environments


---

## 5. Risk Concerns（购买风险）


Address:


- Common problems
- Limitations
- Return issues
- Maintenance
- Warranty


Never hide disadvantages.


---

# Buying Guide Structure（购买指南结构）


Every guide should follow:


---

# H1 Title


Example:


Best [Product Category] for [Target Audience] in 2026


Must include:


- Product category
- Target audience
- Search intent


---

# Introduction（介绍）


Explain:


- What buyers are looking for
- Common problems
- How this guide helps


Do not start with product promotion.


---

# Quick Recommendation Summary（快速推荐）


Provide:


Best Overall

Best Budget

Best Premium

Best For Beginners

Best Value


Explain WHY.


---

# Buyer Considerations（购买前需要考虑）


This section is mandatory.


Explain:


## Budget


What price range means.


Example:


Lower price:

Basic features


Higher price:

Better quality and performance


---

## Usage Scenario


Ask:


How will you use it?


Examples:


Daily use

Professional use

Outdoor use

Family use


---

## Important Features


Explain:


Which specifications matter.


Example:


Battery capacity matters because...


Motor power matters because...


---

## Features That Do Not Matter As Much


Help users avoid marketing traps.


Example:


Higher numbers do not always mean better performance.


---

# Product Selection Methodology（选品方法）


Explain:


How products are selected.


Example:


We evaluate:


- Performance
- Value
- User feedback
- Reliability
- Features
- Price


---

# Product Recommendation Rules（产品推荐规则）


Every recommended product must include:


## Product Name


## Best For


Who should buy it.


## Main Advantages


Why recommend it.


## Limitations


Who should avoid it.


## Key Specifications


Only important information.


---

# Honest Ranking Rules（真实排名规则）


Do not rank products only because:


- Higher commission
- Brand request
- Higher price


Ranking should be based on:


User benefit.


---

# Comparison Table（对比表）


Include:


| Product | Best For | Main Strength | Main Weakness | Price Level |
|---|---|---|---|---|


Help users quickly compare.


---

# "Who Should Buy" Section（适合人群）


Every product needs:


Recommended for:


Example:


Good choice for:


- Beginners
- Budget buyers
- Professional users


---

# "Who Should Avoid" Section（不适合人群）


Mandatory.


Explain:


Who may regret buying it.


Examples:


Not suitable for:


- Users needing premium features
- Users with different requirements


---

# Common Buying Mistakes（常见购买错误）


Every buying guide should include:


Examples:


Mistake 1:

Choosing only by price


Mistake 2:

Ignoring long-term cost


Mistake 3:

Buying unnecessary features


Mistake 4:

Ignoring user needs


---

# FAQ（购买问题）


Questions should reflect real buyer concerns.


Examples:


What should I look for before buying?


How much should I spend?


Which features are important?


Is this product worth it?


---

# Affiliate Rules（联盟规则）


Recommendations must be:


Honest

Balanced

User-focused


Do not:


Use aggressive sales language.


Avoid:


"Buy now before it sells out"


unless it is a verified real situation.


---

# Trust Building（建立信任）


Include:


- Research method
- Evaluation criteria
- Pros and cons
- Limitations


Explain:


Why readers should trust this guide.


---

# SEO Requirements（SEO要求）


Every buying guide needs:


Primary keyword


Secondary keywords


FAQ keywords


Internal links


Schema:


- Article Schema
- FAQ Schema


---

# Content Quality Standard（质量标准）


A good buying guide should help users answer:


1. What type of product do I need?


2. How much should I spend?


3. Which features matter?


4. Which products fit my situation?


5. What mistakes should I avoid?


---

# Final Principle（最终原则）


The best buying guide is not the one that sells the most expensive product.


The best buying guide is the one that helps users make the best decision.


最好的购买指南不是卖出最贵产品。


而是真正帮助用户买到适合自己的产品。
