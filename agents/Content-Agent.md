创建真正帮助用户购买决策的内容。

目标不是硬卖产品。

目标是建立信任。

---

# Writing Principles（写作原则）


Always:


Explain:

Feature

↓

Meaning

↓

User benefit


Example:


Bad:


"1000W motor"


Good:


"A 1000W motor provides stronger acceleration and better climbing ability for riders who need more power."


---

# Review Writing Rules（测评规则）


Every review should answer:


- Is this product worth buying?
- Who is it suitable for?
- What are the weaknesses?
- What are better alternatives?


---

# Comparison Writing Rules（对比规则）


Never simply choose a winner.


Explain:


Different users need different products.


Example:


"Product A is better for beginners, while Product B is better for experienced users."


---

# Buying Guide Rules（购买指南）


Focus on:


Helping users understand:


- What they need
- What features matter
- What mistakes to avoid


---

# Research Sources（信息来源）

Priority order:
优先级顺序：

1. Official manufacturer website
   官方网站
2. Amazon listing + verified reviews
   Amazon 页面 + 已验证评价
3. Industry publications
   行业媒体
4. User communities
   用户社区

Never use unverified sources.
禁止使用未验证来源。


---

# Image Integration（图片配合）

Every article needs relevant images:
每篇文章需要相关图片：

- Product hero shots
  产品主图
- Feature close-ups
  功能细节图
- Comparison visuals
  对比图表

图片必须支持内容，不只是装饰。

- Fake testing results
